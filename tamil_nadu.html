Timeless Tamil Nadu

Destinations: Chennai(1) - Mahabalipuram(2) - Pondicherry(2) - Tanjore(1) - Madurai(2) - Rameswaram(1)

Duration: 11 Nights / 12 Days

Highlights

  • Chennai City Tour covering Fort St George, Marina Beach, Santhome Church and Mylapore temple.
  • Mahabalipuram UNESCO World Heritage sites
  • Explore Sri Aurobindo Ashram in Pondicherry
  • Visit Auroville with Solar Kitchen lunch
  • Visit to World Heritage Site of Brihadeeswarar Temple in Tanjore and Palace
  • Chettinad Mansions and handicrafts
  • Visit Madurai Meenakshi Amman temple, Thirumalai Nayakkar Mahal and foodies tour
  • Ramanathaswamy temple and Dhanushkodi.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive in Chennai - Visit Fort St. George, Marina Beach, Santhome Church, and Mylapore Temple. Overnight in Chennai.

Day 2-3: Explore Kalakshetra Foundation in Chennai. Proceed to Mahabalipuram for UNESCO sites. Leisure and local exploration. Overnight in Mahabalipuram.

Day 4-5: Drive to Pondicherry - Explore French & Tamil Quarters, Sri Aurobindo Ashram, Auroville, and Solar Kitchen lunch. Overnight in Pondicherry.

Day 6: Visit Gangaikonda Cholapuram & Airavatesvara Temple en route to Tanjore. Explore Brihadeeswarar Temple. Overnight in Tanjore.

Day 7-8: Visit Chettinad Mansions, Athangudi tiles workshop, and local markets in Karaikudi. Overnight in Karaikudi.

Day 9-10: Explore Meenakshi Amman Temple, Thirumalai Nayakkar Mahal, and local cuisine in Madurai. Overnight in Madurai.

Day 11: Visit Ramanathaswamy Temple and Dhanushkodi in Rameswaram. Overnight in Rameswaram.

Day 12: Return to Madurai for final sightseeing/shopping. Departure from Madurai.
