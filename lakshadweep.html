Paradise of Lakshadweep

DESTINATIONS: BANGARAM ISLAND
DURATION: 5 NIGHTS / 6 DAYS

  • Stunning Location: Surrounded by pristine beaches and turquoise waters, offering a serene escape.
  • Accommodation: Beach cottages and luxury tents blending with natural surroundings.
  • Water Activities: Snorkeling, scuba diving, kayaking, and paddleboarding for vibrant marine life encounters.
  • Relaxation and Wellness: Wellness treatments, spa services, and yoga for a holistic experience.
  • Culinary Delights: Local and international cuisine with beachfront dining options.
  • Natural Beauty: Stunning coral reefs, white sandy beaches, and lush coconut palms.
  • Privacy and Exclusivity: Limited accommodations for a tranquil atmosphere.
  • Eco-Friendly Practices: Commitment to sustainability to preserve natural beauty.
  • Exploration Opportunities: Guided tours to nearby uninhabited islands.

Day 1

Arrival at Agatti Island Airport, followed by a scenic jetty drive and speedboat transfer to Bangaram Island. Check-in at the resort, relax, and enjoy local and international cuisine.

Day 2 - 5

Daily breakfast followed by water sports activities (payment on a direct basis) including snorkeling, scuba diving, kayaking, and paddleboarding. Leisure time to relax on the beach. Enjoy delicious lunch and dinner at the resort.

Day 6

Final breakfast and check-out. Speedboat transfer back to Agatti Island. End of services.
