Karnataka & Goa: The Perfect Duo

Destinations: Mumbai(2) - Goa(3) - Badami(3) - Hampi(2) - Mysore(2)

Duration: 12 Nights / 13 Days

Highlights

  • Visit the sights of Mumbai- Gateway of India and Elephanta Caves.
  • Visit the Belgaum Fort.
  • Explore Pattadakal and Aihole.
  • Discover the World Heritage Site of Hampi.
  • Excursion to Belur and Halebid, showcasing Hoyasala architecture.
  • See the monolithic statue of Lord Bahubali - 17 meters high.
  • Visit the Sri Jayachamarajendra Art Gallery & Jagmohan Palace.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrive in Mumbai, check-in, and explore Gateway of India, Colaba Causeway, and Marine Drive. Overnight in Mumbai.

Day 2: Visit Elephanta Caves, Dhobi Ghat, and Kala Ghoda. Overnight in Mumbai.

Day 3: Fly to Goa, check-in, and relax. Overnight in Goa.

Day 4: Leisure day in Goa. Enjoy beach time, spa, or resort amenities. Overnight in Goa.

Day 5: Heritage walk in Old Goa and spice plantation tour. Overnight in Goa.

Day 6: Drive to Badami, with a stop at Belgaum Fort. Overnight in Badami.

Day 7: Explore Badami rock-cut cave temples and Bhuthanatha Temples. Overnight in Badami.

Day 8: Day trip to Aihole and Pattadakal. Overnight in Badami.

Day 9: Drive to Hampi, evening heritage visit. Overnight in Hampi.

Day 10: Explore Hampi's key sites including Lotus Mahal and Elephant Stables. Overnight on board to Mysore.

Day 11: Arrive in Mysore, visit Mysore Palace. Overnight in Mysore.

Day 12: Visit Chamundi Hill, explore local markets. Overnight in Mysore.

Day 13: Drive to Bangalore via Shravanabelagola, transfer to Bangalore Airport. Tour Ends.
