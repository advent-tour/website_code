Karnataka & Goa: The Perfect Duo

Discover the Perfect Blend of Heritage and Beaches on a 12-night, 13-day journey through Mumbai, Goa, Badami, Hampi, and Mysore. Explore historical monuments, serene beaches, and rich cultural heritage.

Begin in Mumbai with visits to the Gateway of India and the Elephanta Caves.

Proceed to Goa for pristine beaches, a heritage walk in Old Goa, and a spice plantation tour.

In Badami, explore the rock-cut cave temples and visit the Belgaum Fort.

Discover the ancient ruins of Hampi, including the Vijaya Vittala Temple, Lotus Mahal, and Queen's Bath.

Visit Mysore to see the magnificent Mysore Palace, Chamundi Hill, and the Jagmohan Palace.

Conclude with a visit to Shravanabelagola to witness the towering Lord Bahubali statue before departing from Bangalore.

End this enriching journey with unforgettable memories of Karnataka and Goa.
